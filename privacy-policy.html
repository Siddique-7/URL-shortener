Privacy Policy for Tiny Urls

At Tiny Urls, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our URL shortening service. By using our platform, you agree to the terms and conditions described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Tiny Urls, we may collect certain personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal Information: We do not require you to provide personal information to use our URL shortening service. However, if you choose to contact us, we may collect your name, email address, and any other information you voluntarily provide in communication with us.
  • Usage Data: We collect non-personal information about how you interact with our website, including your IP address, browser type, device type, referral URLs, and the pages you visit. This data helps us improve our website and service.
  • Cookies: Like many websites, Tiny Urls uses cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device to track your activities on our site. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect some functionalities of our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Improve Our Service: We use the collected data to ensure the proper functioning of our URL shortening service and to improve our website's performance, security, and usability.
  • To Monitor Usage: We analyze the data to understand how users interact with our service, which helps us to enhance user experience and troubleshoot any issues.
  • For Communication: If you contact us, we may use your email address to respond to your inquiries, resolve issues, or provide support.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: We may use or disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, and safety of Tiny Urls, our users, or others.

3. How We Protect Your Information

Your privacy is very important to us, and we take the protection of your information seriously. We use a variety of security measures to protect your personal data, including encryption, secure servers, and firewalls. However, no data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, so while we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. However, we may share non-personal, aggregated data with trusted third parties, such as analytics providers, to help us improve our service. This data does not personally identify you.

Tiny Urls may also use third-party service providers to perform certain functions on our behalf, such as hosting services or email marketing. These third-party providers are only given access to the data necessary to perform their functions, and they are required to protect the confidentiality of your information.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other websites. Please note that these third-party sites have their own privacy policies, which we do not control. We recommend that you review the privacy policies of these external sites before sharing any personal information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. If you would like to review or update any personal information you have provided to us, please contact us at help@abdulajij.in. If you no longer wish to receive communications from us, you can unsubscribe from our emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in the email.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or service offerings. When we make updates, we will post the updated policy on this page and update the "last updated" date at the bottom of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please do not hesitate to reach out to us at help@abdulajij.in.

Thank you for trusting Tiny Urls with your URL shortening needs. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.
