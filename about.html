About Tiny Urls

Make long URLs short, shareable, and trackable in seconds.

Our Mission

We aim to simplify link management for everyone. From individuals to businesses, Tiny Urls provides a fast, free, and reliable service to shorten, customize, and analyze URLs with ease.

Why Choose Us?

  • Custom short links with branding
  • Real-time analytics and tracking
  • Free forever, no hidden fees
  • Clean and secure experience

How It Works

  1. Paste your long URL into the homepage form
  2. Customize your short link (optional)
  3. Click "Shorten" and get your new URL instantly
  4. Track link performance with our dashboard
